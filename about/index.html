About Me

I am a high school senior who enjoys anything and everything STEM - from space and astronomy to computer programming. I am also a runner, baseball player, and I listen to a lot of music (mostly Muse).

My largest project to date is a word game called Fives. Fives consists of over 1,200 lines of JavaScript and jQuery and is still in active development. Check it out!

I am a two time All-State and First Team All-Conference cross country runner and I was the right fielder on the 2014 Class S State Championship baseball team. My best 5K time is 16:53.7 and my best mile time is 4:47.

I founded a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team, the Portland "Cybernetics" in my Junior year, and during our first season of competition, we made it to the final match of the State Championship tournament (more).

My favorite gadget is my Raspberry Pi that I use to host a local webserver for development and manage my Github repos.
